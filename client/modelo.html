Classification Model

A classification model is used to predict the class or category of an input. It is commonly used in tasks such as image recognition, spam detection, and sentiment analysis.

Regression Model

A regression model is used to predict a continuous numerical value, such as the price of a house or the sales of a product. It is commonly used in tasks such as forecasting, stock market prediction, and customer churn analysis.

Clustering Model

A clustering model is used to group similar data points together, without any prior knowledge of the classes or categories. It is commonly used in tasks such as customer segmentation, anomaly detection, and market analysis.

Natural Language Processing Model

A natural language processing (NLP) model is used to analyze and understand human language, such as text or speech. It is commonly used in tasks such as sentiment analysis, language translation, and text summarization.

Computer Vision Model

A computer vision model is used to analyze and understand digital images and videos. It is commonly used in tasks such as object detection, image classification, and facial recognition.
